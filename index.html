About

I am an incoming PhD student at City University of Hong Kong (CityUHK), affiliated with the Hong Kong Institute of AI for Science. My current research focuses on medical AI, computational social science, and complex systems.

I am advised by Prof Jun YAN, Dr Yao WANG, and Prof Maolin WANG. Before this, I had the privilege of being mentored by Prof Hao PENG in the Department of Data Science at CityUHK.

Research Focus

My current research interests span the following three directions:

Medical AI

  • Medical LLM reasoning
  • Knowledge Graph (KG)
  • Automated LLM Agents
  • EHR dataset analysis

Computational Social Science

  • Innovation dynamics

Complex Systems

  • Social physics
  • Information diffusion
  • Infectious disease modeling
